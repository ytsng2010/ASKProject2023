Atkinson Clock Tower

  • built to commemorate Jesselton's first District Officer, Francis George Atkinson, who died after being attacked by malaria when he was only 28 years old.
  • His mother, Mary Edith, gave a two-faced clock for this building. However, the original building that was completed in 1905 was replaced by a new building in 1964.
